DESCRIPTION (4000 chars max)
========================================
Kala is the photo journal you'll actually use every day. 

Designed for people who want to document their lives without the noise of social media or the privacy concerns of big tech. Kala turns daily photo-taking into a calming, intentional ritual—and keeps it entirely offline.
No likes. No followers. No algorithms. Just you, your camera, and a beautiful calendar that fills with memories over time.

◆ LOCAL-FIRST PRIVACY
Your photos and notes shouldn't live on someone else's server. Kala stores everything locally on your device. 
  • No Accounts: Start journaling immediately.
  • No Tracking: We don't collect a single byte of data.
  • No Cloud: Your phone is your personal memory vault.

◆ CAPTURE ONE MOMENT EVERY DAY
Take a photo, add a note, and move on. Kala's one-day-at-a-time philosophy helps you stay present while building a rich visual diary. Capture up to 8 photos per day—and organize them into titled sections for those days that deserve a little extra context.

◆ A CALENDAR THAT TELLS YOUR STORY
Your home screen is a beautifully minimal calendar that fills with photo thumbnails as you capture memories. Swipe between months, tap on any day to revisit or add entries, and watch your life unfold at a glance.

◆ TWO BEAUTIFUL THEMES
Choose the look that fits your style:
  • Minimal — Photo previews fill each calendar day for a rich, magazine-like grid.
  • Super Minimal — Elegant dots represent captured days for a more abstract, zen-like view. Hold to peek at dates.

◆ GESTURE-DRIVEN NAVIGATION
Everything flows naturally. Swipe up and down to scroll between months. Swipe on the year to jump through time. Double-tap the Home tab to snap back to today—with a satisfying pulse animation.

◆ YOUR DATA STAYS YOURS
Kala stores everything locally on your device. No accounts. No cloud sync. No tracking. Your memories belong to you and you alone.

◆ EXPORT YOUR JOURNEY
Export any month's entries as beautifully organized Markdown files—each day gets its own folder with a .md file and all associated photos. Perfect for personal blogs, backup archives, or printing your own memory book.

◆ MULTI-USER SUPPORT
Share a device with family? Each person can have their own journal space with a personalized avatar. Switch between profiles with a tap from the Settings screen.

◆ DARK MODE & LIGHT MODE
Use Kala in Light, Dark, or System mode. Every screen adapts gracefully so your journal looks stunning day and night.

◆ PREMIUM TOUCHES EVERYWHERE
Haptic feedback on every interaction. Smooth spring-based animations powered by React Native Reanimated. A floating action button with a drag-to-select gesture for capturing photos. Every pixel has been considered.

Start your daily photo journal today. Private, local, and built for your memories. Your future self will thank you.
